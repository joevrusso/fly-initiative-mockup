Who We Are

We See the Value
in Everyone.

Since 2017, FLY has been working to end generational poverty by giving young people the financial knowledge, confidence, and real-world skills they need to build a different kind of future.

We go into schools, community organizations, and partner programs and do the work ourselves. Culturally responsive. Fully delivered. No extra burden on your team.

What We Do

How We Show Up
for Students

Not a worksheet. Not a one-day workshop. A real program with trained people behind it.

01
Financial Literacy Education

Standards-aligned curriculum taught by FLY educators in classrooms, afterschool programs, and community settings. Students learn by doing, not by listening.

02
Mentorship & Coaching

One-on-one and group connections with professionals who have been where these students are going. Real relationships. Honest conversations.

03
Community Outreach

Financial education does not stop at the classroom door. FLY brings programming into communities, families, and events where young people actually are.

04
Partner Programs

Schools, nonprofits, and institutions bring FLY in and we handle the rest. Curriculum, educators, materials, and delivery. You open the door. We do the work.
